OUR STORY

Previous Next

Kelley Bros. Disposal is a family owned and operated business. Established in 2016, the Kelley family purchased this garbage disposal with the goal of providing reasonable rates and reliable service. The owner, Cody Kelley, was born and raised in Pittston, Maine. Today, he still resides there, raising a family of his own. He uses the lessons learned from a life of farming and construction to bring a strong work ethic and personal service to the garbage disposal industry. Cody is a member of the community he serves. His goal is to make your visits from the “trash man” the same happy and fond encounters he remembers from his own childhood.

Kelley Bros. Disposal strives to help each customer in each and every situation, by providing what you need, and at the best price. Our company now offers roll off services for larger cleanout and demo needs, and as always, continues to serve our residential customers with curbside pickup and rear load dumpsters. We strive for excellence, doing our best to always leave a clean area with trash cans and covers together.

PROUDLY SERVING THE CENTRAL MAINE AREA

Pittston, Dresden, Richmond, Bowdoinham, Whitefield, Chelsea, Augusta, Hallowell, Farmingdale, Gardiner, and West Gardiner
